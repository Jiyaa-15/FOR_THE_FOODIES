Laal Maans

Ingredients

  • Mutton (500 g)
  • Dried red chillies (soaked & ground)
  • Yogurt (curd)
  • Onions (finely chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Mustard oil
  • Red chilli powder (Kashmiri)
  • Turmeric powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Whole spices (cloves, bay leaf, cinnamon)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Recipe

  1. Soak dried red chillies in warm water and grind into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat mustard oil until smoking, then let it cool slightly.
  3. Add whole spices and saut√© until aromatic.
  4. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell disappears.
  6. Add mutton pieces and sear well.
  7. Lower the flame and add yogurt gradually, stirring continuously.
  8. Add chilli paste, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat until mutton is tender.
  10. Finish with garam masala and garnish with coriander.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken (500 g)
  • Onions (thinly sliced)
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chillies
  • Yogurt (curd)
  • Red chilli powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garam masala
  • Biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Mint leaves
  • Cooking oil / ghee
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)

Recipe

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chilli powder, turmeric, biryani masala, and salt. Rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and fry sliced onions until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook until chicken is tender.
  6. In a separate pot, boil water with whole spices and salt. Add rice and cook till 70% done.
  7. Layer the cooked rice over the chicken mixture.
  8. Add mint and coriander leaves on top.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 10‚Äì15 minutes.
  10. Gently mix and serve hot with raita.
